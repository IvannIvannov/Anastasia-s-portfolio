UGC

Content Creation

Depending on the agreement with the client, I create content for a specific product—both videos and photos. I work with high-quality equipment and guarantee professional results. All footage is edited and sent to the client for approval. After final adjustments, the content is delivered via WeTransfer or a shared album to preserve its quality. For videos, I select a suitable thumbnail that fits the brand’s feed and aesthetics when publishing on social media.

Music

I choose music based on the theme and mood of the video, usually from Reels suggestions. If the client has specific music preferences or wants to use a particular track, they simply send it over. I'm also open to exploring new methods to make the process easier and more convenient for the client.

Voiceover

I can record a thematic voiceover for the video. The script can either be provided by the brand or created by me. If the video will be published on my personal profile, I may make slight adjustments to the script to better reflect my personal style. The voiceover can be recorded in English, German, or Bulgarian.

Captions and Hashtags (upon request)

If requested, I can write the caption and choose relevant hashtags. I discuss the main message the client wants to communicate, send a draft for approval, and make any necessary edits. Based on the final caption, I then create fitting and strategic hashtags.
