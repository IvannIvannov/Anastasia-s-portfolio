ABOUT ME

My name is Anastasia Paskaleva, I’m 21 years old and from Plovdiv. I graduated from the National High School of Trade and Commerce with a major in Banking, and I am currently a second-year student at the Technical University of Sofia, specializing in Business Informatics (Wirtschaftsinformatik).

I've been drawn to social media since I was a child, which led me to create my own YouTube channel where I share moments from my daily life and aim to be helpful to others. That’s where my passion for video editing was born.

Wanting to grow my presence on social media, I started looking for courses on the topic. I’ve completed many, including "Instaboom", "Instasell", "MyStories", “InstaUGS”, inStories, the “Blogger as a Profession” marathon, “Storymaker”, and others.

Interests

Graphic Design

I've been working in graphic design for two years, creating presentations for courses, marathons, and webinars, designing logos for brands and businesses, and crafting creative templates for guides, handbooks, e-books, and more.

Video Editing

My passion for video editing began over five years ago, but I started pursuing it more professionally just over a year ago. I edit videos for all social media platforms, most frequently for YouTube and Instagram (reels). I create content for brands and individuals who want to grow their presence on social media.

Social Media Management

For about a year now, I’ve been managing social media profiles—a role I truly enjoy. I mentor a few girls, helping them maintain and grow their profiles, and I also work with brands whose accounts I manage entirely.

Content Creation for Social Media

I’ve been creating content for social media for over two years. It all started with helping friends who owned small businesses by shooting product photos for their websites. Over time, what began as a favor turned into a hobby and eventually grew into a source of income.
