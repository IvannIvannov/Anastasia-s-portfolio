ABOUT ME

My name is Anastasia Paskaleva, I’m 21 years old and from Plovdiv. I graduated from the National High School of Trade and Commerce with a major in Banking, and I am currently a second-year student at the Technical University of Sofia, specializing in Business Informatics (Wirtschaftsinformatik).

I've been drawn to social media since I was a child, which led me to create my own YouTube channel where I share moments from my daily life and aim to be helpful to others. That’s where my passion for video editing was born.

Wanting to grow my presence on social media, I started looking for courses on the topic. I’ve completed many, including "Instaboom", "Instasell", "MyStories", “InstaUGS”, inStories, the “Blogger as a Profession” marathon, “Storymaker”, and others.
